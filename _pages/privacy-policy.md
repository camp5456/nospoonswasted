*Last updated: July 2026*

Your privacy matters here. This policy explains what information No Spoons Wasted collects, how it is used, and what rights you have. It is written in plain language because legal jargon wastes spoons.

---

## About this site

**No Spoons Wasted** is a blog and newsletter for fellow spoonies and those who struggle with low-energy living whether due to illness or some other reason. Questions about this policy can be directed to the [contact form](/contact/).

---

## What this site collects and why

### Email address
If you sign up for the No Spoons Wasted newsletter, your email address is collected. It is used to send a weekly newsletter with recent blog posts, easy recipes, products that save you spoons, and other resources to support the spoonie community. That's it. It is not sold, shared, or used for anything else.

Your email is stored and managed through MailerLite. You can read their [privacy policy](https://www.mailerlite.com/privacy-policy){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" aria-label="MailerLite privacy policy (opens in new tab)"}.

You can unsubscribe at any time using the link at the bottom of every email.

### Analytics
This site uses Matomo Analytics, a privacy-friendly analytics platform. Matomo is self-hosted, which means your data stays on No Spoons Wasted's server and is never shared with third parties.

Matomo collects basic information about your visit — pages viewed, time on site, approximate location (country/region level), browser type, and device type. This helps No Spoons Wasted understand what content is useful so more of it can be made. It cannot in any way be used to identify you personally. It has been completely anonymized.

Matomo is configured to run in cookieless mode, which means no tracking cookies are set and no personal information is collected through analytics. Because of this, no cookie consent banner is displayed — there is nothing to consent to.

Matomo does not track you across other websites. No Spoons Wasted does not use Google Analytics or any other third-party tracking service.

### Contact form
If you use the contact form, your name and email address are collected in order to respond, along with any other information you voluntarily provide. You are not added to the mailing list when you use the contact form. Joining the mailing list is your choice entirely and can be done on several pages of this site including the home page.

Contact form submissions are processed through Formspree. They do not sell your data and retain it for 30 days only. You can read their [privacy policy](https://formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" aria-label="Formspree privacy policy (opens in new tab)"}.

### Cookies
This site does not use tracking or analytics cookies. Matomo, the analytics platform used by this site, is configured to track visits without setting any cookies.

No advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies are used. No cookie consent banner is required or displayed on this site.

## Who your data is shared with

Your data is shared with no one, with the following limited exceptions:

- **MailerLite** — stores your email to send you a weekly newsletter. They are GDPR compliant.
- **Formspree** — processes contact form submissions. They do not sell data and are GDPR compliant. They delete your form submissions after 30 days.
- **SiteGround** — hosts this website. Your page visits and general site traffic pass through their servers. They are GDPR compliant. You can read their [privacy policy](https://www.siteground.com/privacy.htm){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer" aria-label="SiteGround privacy policy (opens in new tab)"}.
- **Legal requirements** — information may be disclosed if required by law.

No Spoons Wasted does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Ever.

## Your rights

You have rights that No Spoons Wasted acknowledges and protects.

- **Access** the personal information No Spoons Wasted holds about you (email address)
- **Correct** any inaccurate information
- **Delete** your information. [Contact us](/contact/) to request your email be deleted from MailerLite.
- **Unsubscribe** from the newsletter at any time using the link in any email. Please keep in mind MailerLite doesn't delete your email if you unsubscribe through the system. It sets your status as inactive. To be fully deleted, please use the [contact form](/contact/) and your email will be deleted from MailerLite.
- **Opt out of Matomo analytics** — because cookieless tracking is used, no personal data is collected through analytics. If you'd prefer not to be counted at all, you can enable Do Not Track in your browser settings because Matomo respects this setting. A quick search with the search phrase "Turn on Do Not Track on [your browser's name]" will help you do this.

To exercise any of these rights, use the [contact form](/contact/). Responses are sent within 5 business days.

## Data retention

Your email address is kept for as long as you are subscribed to the newsletter. When you unsubscribe, MailerLite marks you as inactive. You can always request complete deletion. Please review the "Your Rights" section on this page for more information about how to do that.

Contact form submissions are kept for 30 days only. After 30 days, Formspree will automatically delete anything submitted through the contact form. This also means correspondence can only be referenced within the last 30 days.

## Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. No personal information from children is knowingly collected. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please use the [contact form](/contact/) and it will be deleted.
