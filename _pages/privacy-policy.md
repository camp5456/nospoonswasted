Your privacy matters here. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have. We've written it in plain language because legal jargon wastes spoons.

---

## Who we are

No Spoons Lifehacks is a blog and newsletter for the chronic illness community, at nospoonslifehacks.com. You can reach us via our [contact form](/contact/) with any questions about this policy.

---

## What we collect and why

### Email address
If you sign up for the No Spoons Wasted newsletter, we collect your email address. We use it to send you the weekly product roundup you signed up for. That's it. We don't sell it, share it, or use it for anything else.

Your email is stored and managed through MailerLite. You can read their privacy policy at mailerlite.com/privacy-policy.

You can unsubscribe at any time using the link at the bottom of every email. We won't take it personally.

### Analytics
This site uses Matomo Analytics, a privacy-friendly analytics platform. Matomo is self-hosted, which means your data stays on our server and is never shared with third parties.

Matomo collects basic information about your visit — pages viewed, time on site, approximate location (country/region level), browser type, and device type. This helps us understand what content is useful so we can make more of it.

Matomo does not track you across other websites. We do not use Google Analytics or any other third-party tracking service.

### Contact form
If you use our contact form, we collect your name and email address in order to respond to you. We don't add you to our mailing list without your explicit consent.

### Cookies
Matomo uses a first-party cookie to distinguish between visits. This cookie does not contain personal information and is not used for advertising. You can opt out of Matomo tracking below.

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

---

## Affiliate links

This site and the No Spoons Wasted newsletter contain affiliate links, primarily through the Amazon Associates program. If you click a link and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

We only recommend products we genuinely believe are useful for the chronic illness community. Affiliate commissions help keep this site running.

---

## Who we share your data with

We share your data with no one, with the following limited exceptions:

- **MailerLite** — stores and sends our newsletter. They are GDPR compliant.
- **SiteGround** — hosts this website. Your data passes through their servers.
- **Legal requirements** — we may disclose information if required by law.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Ever.

---

## Your rights

You have the right to:

- **Access** the personal information we hold about you
- **Correct** any inaccurate information
- **Delete** your information — use our [contact form](/contact/) and we will remove you from all our systems
- **Unsubscribe** from our newsletter at any time using the link in any email
- **Opt out of Matomo analytics** by enabling Do Not Track in your browser settings — Matomo respects this setting

To exercise any of these rights, use our [contact form](/contact/). We will respond within 30 days.

---

## Data retention

We keep your email address for as long as you are subscribed to our newsletter. When you unsubscribe we remove your address from our active list. You can request complete deletion at any time.

Contact form submissions are kept only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry.

---

## Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please use our [contact form](/contact/) and we will delete it.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. When we do, we will update the date at the top of this page. We will not reduce your rights without giving you notice.
