*Last updated: May 2026*

**No Spoons Wasted** participates in the Amazon Associates Program and other affiliate programs. This means that when you click a link to a product on the blog or newsletter and make a purchase, No Spoons Wasted may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

---

## The real part

This site exists for one reason: to help fellow spoonies live the best life possible on whatever energy they have that day. Affiliate links are how this site keeps the lights on without plastering it with ads.

No Spoons Wasted is a fierce advocate of your privacy, and ad infrastructure can't protect it. Nobody likes ads. That's why this site is self-sustaining with affiliate links.

Only products that are genuinely believed to be useful for the community are recommended here. This business model is beneficial for everyone — it supports the community with quality content at no added cost and without predatory marketing or business practices.

---

## The content part

The blogs and the newsletter are made by a human. So no super smooth AI text over here. You're getting the messy human stuff — warts and all. There will be typos. Don't worry, everyone survives a typo.

---

## What this means for you

- Recommendations are genuine.
- If something didn't work as hoped, the post will say so.
- Clicking these links costs you nothing extra and helps keep this site going without selling your eyeballs to advertisers.
- You are under no obligation to use these links. No Spoons Wasted won't know either way. Promise.

## Partnership Requests

No Spoons Wasted accepts free products for honest reviews. If you'd like to send products or services to try, that is welcome.

**Payments in exchange for a good review are not accepted.**

If a product doesn't seem helpful, no content about it will be published. This blog has no interest in publishing negative or critical reviews.

If a product seems helpful to other spoonies, No Spoons Wasted will reach out about an affiliate partnership where appropriate. Once a partnership is established, an honest review will be published.

Partnerships of this nature generate revenue that keeps this site self-sufficient.

If you are interested in this form of partnership, [get in touch](/contact/) to start a conversation.
